### F. Platform Preferences by Firm Size/Type

**Large firms (50+ advisers / consolidators):**
- Increasingly consolidating onto 1-3 preferred platforms
- Negotiate commercial terms with platforms
- 24% of firms with £250m+ AUA have own white-label platform
- Consolidator activity driving flows to Quilter, Aviva, Transact (standardise post-acquisition)
- More strategically selective; drive large concentrated flows

**Small/independent IFA firms:**
- Parmenion specifically targets this segment: 1,373 firms using platform (avg ~9 advisers/firm)
- Parmenion model appeals as integrated platform + risk-profiled investment solution = low overhead
- "Recommended by more UK advisers than any other wrap platform" (Investment Trends 2025)
- More likely to still use Transact for wealthier/more complex clients
- Platform Shakeout research: smaller firms still consolidating away from multiple platforms

**Restricted/network-tied firms:**
- True Potential (10th by Defaqto recommendations) primarily operates within own restricted network
- Quilter has integrated adviser network (Quilter Financial Planning) driving significant flows

**By platform type used:**
- The "top 3" (Quilter/Aviva/Transact) are used by all firm types but capture disproportionate share from larger/consolidating firms
- Parmenion, Nucleus, Fundment more associated with independent/smaller/quality-focused firms

## 3. FCA Data Assessment

The FCA Retail Intermediary Market Data 2024 (published Nov 2025, Open Government Licence) is available for free download at:
https://www.fca.org.uk/data/retail-intermediary-market-data-2024

**What it contains:**
- Number of adviser firms by type (independent, restricted)
- Number of adviser posts
- Revenue from retail investment intermediation
- Capital requirements distribution
- Interactive dashboards

**What it does NOT contain:**
- No platform-by-platform market share data
- No breakdown of which platforms are used by which firm types
- No data on flows per platform
- No data on platform AUM

The FCA RMAR does not ask firms to report which platforms they use. Platform usage data must come from commercial research sources (Lang Cat, Fundscape, Platforum, Defaqto, NextWealth, Investment Trends).

## 4. Key Statistics Summary

- **Market size:** £616bn advised platform AUM end-2024; ~£991bn end-2025
- **Concentration:** Top 6 platforms = 60% of gross flows; top 3 = ~50%+ of net flows
- **Quilter dominance in 2025:** £8.7bn net flows = ~half of all advised platform net flows
- **Adviser recommendations (Defaqto):** Aviva 29%, Quilter 28%, Fidelity 9%, Transact 7%, AJ Bell 6%
- **Analyser popularity (Lang Cat):** Transact #1, then Quilter, AJ Bell, Fundment, Aviva
- **Platform consolidation by advisers:** 65% use ≤2 platforms (up from 55% in 2018)
- **Parmenion:** 1,373 advice firms, £13.1bn AUM - specialist smaller-firm platform with integrated MPS
- **Scottish Widows:** Biggest mover in 2025 - from 19th to 8th on Lang Cat Analyser
- **Nucleus:** Biggest riser in FE fundinfo ratings - jumped 8 places to 7th
- **Firm count:** 4,292 independent + 584 restricted = ~4,876 advice firms (FCA 2024)
- **Service issues:** 91% of advisers impacted by poor platform service daily; 45% switched platform in 2024

## 5. Recommended Next Steps for Missing Data

### Priority 1: Lang Cat Analyser Everything Subscription
**Cost:** ~£300+VAT/year
**Get:** Full State of the Platform Nation 2024/25 + quarterly scorecards with per-platform AUM, flows, and market share. Best single source for adviser-facing platform data.

### Priority 2: Platforum UK Adviser Platforms Report
**Contact:** platforum.co.uk
**Get:** Quarterly market overview with User Leaderboard showing adviser firm preferences by firm type and size. Includes individual platform profiles.

### Priority 3: NextWealth Platform Shakeout 2025
**Contact:** nextwealth.co.uk
**Get:** Platform usage data split by firm AUA band (the specific "small vs large firm" breakdown requested). Some free extracts available via Embark sponsorship.

### Priority 4: Fundscape Press Releases (Free)
**URL:** fundscape.co.uk/press-releases
**Get:** Q-by-Q press releases (free PDFs) contain summary AUM and flow rankings for top platforms. Subscribe to free press list.

### Priority 5: FCA RMAR Interactive Dashboard
**URL:** fca.org.uk/data/retail-intermediary-market-data-2024/retail-intermediary-market-interactive-analysis-2024
**Get:** Free download of firm-level intermediary data. Can segment by capital requirement (proxy for firm size). Does not have platform names.

### Priority 6: Direct Platform Contact for Numbers
Several platforms publish their adviser firm counts in annual results:
- Parmenion: 1,373 advice firms (public, from annual results)
- Transact: ~7,000 advisers (from company reports - verify)
- Nucleus: number of advice firms in annual report
- AJ Bell: adviser firm count in trading updates
