## Notes on Name Change

Effective **1 October 2025**, Great-West Lifeco transferred investment management of the WS Canada Life Investments range from Canada Life Asset Management ('CLAM') to a new sister entity, **Keyridge Asset Management Limited** ('Keyridge'). The funds were renamed from "WS Canlife [name]" to "WS Keyridge [name]". The ACD (Waystone Management UK Ltd) and ISINs remain unchanged. Keyridge manages over £135bn in AUM with ~300 asset management professionals. Managing Partner: **Mark Giancola**.
