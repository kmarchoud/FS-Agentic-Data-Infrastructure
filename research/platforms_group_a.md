---

## Background Note

Effective **1 October 2025**, Great-West Lifeco transferred part of the investment management business of **Canada Life Asset Management (CLAM)** to **Keyridge Asset Management Limited**. Keyridge unites the European third-party asset management businesses of CLAM, Setanta Asset Management, and Irish Life Investment Managers (~£135bn AUM). The fund legal structure remained under Waystone Management (UK) Limited as ACD, but the investment manager name changed. From **18 February 2026** the funds were formally renamed from "WS Canlife" to "WS Keyridge" across platforms. Fund objectives, risk profiles, and OCFs were unchanged.

## Key Findings

1. **WS Keyridge name is fully adopted** on HL, Fidelity, and Interactive Investor as of the research date.
2. **AJ Bell and Aviva still show legacy names** (WS Canlife / LF Canlife) — the rebrand has not propagated to their public-facing data.
3. **Quilter published a formal name-change notice** (January 2026, effective 18 Feb 2026) — the most transparent documentation of the transition found.
4. **Zurich likely holds funds** via insurer-specific "Canlife Keyridge LS4/LS5" share classes visible on Trustnet, but no direct confirmation from zurich.co.uk.
5. **LV=** does not appear to be a relevant platform for this fund range.
6. **Transact, Nucleus, Standard Life, and Scottish Widows** could not be confirmed due to login-gated or JS-rendered fund lists — further investigation requiring platform access is needed.
7. **Aviva notes** that these Insured Funds are only available within the Aviva Pension wrapper, not ISA or Investment Account.
